Distressed Assets

JLM's Distressed Credit & Opportunistic Investing Division was created to take advantage of volatility and cycles in the marketplace. During the last downturn, JLM acquired over $220 million worth of distressed commercial real estate, senior housing, and corporate aircraft assets. We look for opportunities that offer attractive risk-adjusted returns where we can capitalize on our ability to underwrite, bring capital, and close deals quickly.

Investment Criteria: Our focus is on loans and REO being sold on the secondary markets by banks, insurance companies, holdings companies, government agencies, private investors, and trustees. We buy loans and REO securitized by hard assets such as commercial and multifamily residential real estate, senior housing and corporate aircraft and equipment. We will consider individual assets, as well as portfolios.

One83 Office Park

JLM Financial Investments, formed SW Growth Partners for the acquisition of a portion of the Freescale Campus located at Ed Bluestein Blvd. and Techni Center Dr. in Austin, TX. The property consists of 109 acres and is comprises 935,255 SF of space. The in-place infrastructure makes it a highly desirable complex to be used for: office space, data center, call center, industrial or warehouse use. The buildings are being repurposed and will offer tenants a convenient location, a high-quality product, and low-cost.

Real Estate

JLM's Real Estate division and its principals acquire and manage both fee simple assets and loans in all areas of commercial and residential real estate. We have made recent investments in Texas, Arizona, Colorado, Georgia, Illinois and Florida that include retail, industrial, medical, multifamily, self-storage, flex, senior housing and office buildings.

Acquisition Criteria: We acquire assets nationwide, with a preference in the South, Southeast, Midwest and Atlantic Coastal areas. Our priority assets are multifamily, retail, office, medical, senior housing, industrial, self-storage and flex.

Development Criteria: During opportunistic market cycles, we will pursue ground up development with a special interest in multifamily, high rise luxury condominiums, and high rise luxury office buildings.
